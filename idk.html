Welcome to Osaka

Explore the vibrant culture, cuisine, and sights of Osaka, Japan. From the historical Osaka Castle to the dazzling Dotonbori district, Osaka offers something for everyone.

Top Attractions in Osaka

Osaka Castle

An iconic symbol of Osaka, featuring a beautiful park and museum.

Dotonbori

A lively area known for street food, neon lights, and the famous Glico sign.

Shinsaibashi Shopping Street

A bustling shopping area with countless stores, cafes, and boutiques.
